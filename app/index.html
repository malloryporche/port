Hey there! While always having an infectious passion for technology, I'm a newly minted convert to the world of front end development. I began my journey this past October, 2014, and am a changed woman ever since. After learning how to code with a friend turned mentor, I eagerly began learning all the knowledge I could about front end development via each and every online source I could find...Lynda, codeschool, codeacademy, treehouse, you name it, and I've probably done it!

After getting what I feel is a solid hold on HTML, CSS and jQuery, I'm ready to begin developing! My main focus is to add to my initial skillset and create a stronger foundation within which I may uncover alternate aspects of development which might interest me.

I have several years of experience working in administrative environments and supportive roles, and am looking for employment with an awesome team, working on fun projects, blah blah. If you're interested in hiring a driven, self-motivated, team member for front-end, look no further! For a printable version of my resumé, click below!
